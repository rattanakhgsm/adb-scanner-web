Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

1. Overview

Guardian Pro ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by CHHUON RATTANA, is dedicated to providing security tools for Android. This policy outlines how we handle information in our mobile app and official website.

2. Data Collection

Guardian Pro functions as a local security utility. We DO NOT COLLECT, STORE, OR TRANSMIT any personal identification information (PII) or user data to any external servers. All operations are processed locally on your device.

3. Accessibility Services

Our application utilizes Accessibility Services to provide its core functionality: detecting hidden adware and malicious pop-ups.

  • We only monitor window changes to identify which application is displaying an overlay or popup.
  • No data from this service is ever recorded, uploaded, or shared with third parties.
  • Users must manually grant this permission in device settings.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for functionality:

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: To list installed apps and detect suspicious software.
  • REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES: To facilitate the uninstallation of unwanted adware.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers (like Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
